PUT THE PARTNER / CLIENT LOGOS IN THIS FOLDER
==============================================

Drop the logo files here, then tell Claude and it will wire them into the
"TRUSTED BY BRANDS & BUSINESS OWNERS" strip on the homepage.

NAME THE FILE AFTER THE BRAND
- The FILENAME becomes the name shown beside the logo on the website.
    BrandFoundry.png  ->  BrandFoundry
    StratosCore.jpg   ->  StratosCore
    zenOS.jpg         ->  zenOS
- So spell/capitalise it exactly how the brand should appear.

FORMAT & COLOUR
- Any format (PNG / JPG / SVG), any size. Height ~200px+ is plenty.
- Send them in their normal/original colours — no need to edit anything.
- Every logo is baked into a clean WHITE-ON-TRANSPARENT mark so the strip stays
  black & white and consistent (and flips to black automatically in light mode).
  Both kinds of source work: dark logo on white, or light logo on black.

NOTE
- Very detailed/illustrated logos turn to mush at ~38px tall. A simple mark or
  monogram always reads best. If you have a simplified version, send that one.

Then say: "nailagay ko na ang logos" and Claude will add them to the strip.

Currently live: BrandFoundry, StratosCore, zenOS.
