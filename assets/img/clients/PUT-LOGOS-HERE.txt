PUT THE PARTNER / CLIENT LOGOS IN THIS FOLDER
==============================================

Just drop the logo files here, then tell Claude and it will wire them into the
"TRUSTED BY BRANDS & BUSINESS OWNERS" strip on the homepage.

BEST FORMAT
- PNG with a TRANSPARENT background (or SVG) — this looks cleanest.
- Any reasonable size; height ~200px+ is plenty. They are displayed at 34px tall.
- Name them after the brand, e.g.  acme.png, brandname.png, partner-x.svg

COLOR
- You do NOT need to convert anything to black & white.
  The site automatically renders them GRAYSCALE (black & white) so every logo
  matches the dark theme, and lifts slightly on hover.
- Send them in their normal/original colours — the website handles the rest.

TIP
- Logos that are pure black may look dim on the dark background. If you have a
  white or light version of a logo, send that one instead.

Then say: "nailagay ko na ang logos" and Claude will swap out the placeholders.
You can delete this note afterwards.
